---
title: "Mining with your GPU"
sidebarTitle: "GPU Mining"
---

If you want better performance than the built-in CPU miner, you can mine with your **GPU** using a GPU miner software compatible with XHash. Since Parallax is still in its early days, developers need to adjust Ethminer -- or other Ethash mining softwares -- to add compatibility with Parallax's XHash.

We'll update this guide once a GPU miner is released.
